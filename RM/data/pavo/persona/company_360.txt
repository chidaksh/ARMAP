Our company's technical infrastructure relies on a solid foundation of well-established machine learning models to drive key business functions. This approach allows us to deliver tangible value and actionable insights without the complexities of cutting-edge deep learning. Our focus remains on robust, interpretable models that provide reliable performance for our current needs.
