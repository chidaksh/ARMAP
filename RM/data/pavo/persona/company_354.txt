Our company's technical infrastructure is built to efficiently leverage classic machine learning techniques. We utilize robust, scalable cloud-based platforms to host our data and run our models. For tasks like churn prediction and personalized recommendations, we primarily employ well-established algorithms such as XGBoost and logistic regression. This foundational approach enables us to derive actionable insights and deliver value without the complexity of cutting-edge research.
