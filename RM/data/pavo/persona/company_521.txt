Our company leverages a foundational technical infrastructure that supports our early-stage machine learning initiatives. We primarily utilize robust, well-understood models like XGBoost and logistic regression for critical business functions such as churn prediction and personalized recommendations. These classic algorithms are deployed on a relatively straightforward cloud environment, ensuring reliability and ease of management. Our focus is on delivering tangible business value through these targeted ML applications, building a solid base for future advancements. This setup allows us to efficiently tackle specific problems without the complexity of more advanced deep learning architectures.
