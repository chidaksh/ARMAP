Our company's technical backbone relies on a straightforward yet effective machine learning infrastructure. We leverage well-established models such as XGBoost and logistic regression for core business functions like predicting customer churn and powering personalized product recommendations. These classic algorithms are implemented using readily available libraries, allowing for efficient development and deployment. Our data science team focuses on feature engineering and model tuning to extract maximum value from our datasets, ensuring reliable performance for these critical tasks. This approach allows us to deliver tangible business impact without the complexity of cutting-edge, research-heavy ML solutions.
